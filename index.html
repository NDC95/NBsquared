About.

The inspiration for NB² emerged from my quest to transform my tiny, cluttered room into a beautifully organised sanctuary.

In 2021, I began collecting and repurposing the luxury packaging aka ‘marbled boxes’ from MATCHES, the online fashion retailer and my previous employer, using them as gift packaging. Then came my 'eureka' moment: why not use these boxes for storage and decoration, too? Once I started, there was no turning back; everything goes inside a box as long as I can fit it — makeup, cosmetics, electronics, sunglasses, stationery... you name it! I then let the boxes either shine or blend in with my home décor.

During my three incredible years with the company, I moved to a slightly bigger flat, and unsurprisingly, my collection grew so extensive that my dad even jokingly questioned my shopping habits once – sacré bleu! I am so passionate about these boxes that I am committed to continually redesigning them to be even more beautiful, durable, and functional.

My ultimate goal is to expand the product range across various homeware categories and to offer interior design services, creating a seamless experience and bringing even more pleasure to our homes and lives!
