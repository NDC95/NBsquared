About.

The inspiration for NBsquared came from my own quest to improve my quality of life and overall wellbeing.

My parents and I lost count of the numerous consultations, tests, hours of waiting, and money spent — often in vain — in our search for a solution. Needless to say, also numerous were the moments when tears were shed and hopes were dashed, slowly eroding my self-esteem and confidence.

Over time, I’ve become increasingly aware of the harmful substances in everyday products — cosmetics, food, kitchen utensils, fabrics, etc. — and the lasting effects they have on our health and the environment. As a result, I’ve made it a priority to minimise, if not eliminate, my exposure to them. What a journey it’s been!

Today, my body remains highly sensitive and prone to reacting to even the smallest triggers. However, after years of careful observation and countless trials and errors, I’ve experienced significant improvement.

My mission is to continue learning and refining my habits and routine for an even healthier, happier life, and I truly hope that by sharing with you my journey, I can help you to improve your life as well!
