About Us.

The inspiration for NB² emerged from my quest to turn my tiny, cluttered room into a beautifully organized sanctuary.

In 2021, I began collecting and repurposing the luxurious marbled boxes from MATCHES, an online fashion retailer and my previous employer, using them as gift packaging. Then came my 'eureka' moment: why not use these boxes as storage and decoration, too? Once I started, there was no turning back; everything goes inside a box as long as I can fit it – makeup, cosmetics, electronics, sunglasses, stationery... you name it! I then let the boxes either shine or blend in with my home décor.

Within the three amazing years with the company, I moved to a slightly bigger flat, and, unsurprisingly, my collection grew so extensive that my dad even jokingly questioned my shopping habits once – sacré bleu!

I am so passionate about these boxes that I am committed to continually redesigning them to be even more beautiful, durable, and functional. My goal is to expand the product range across various home accessories categories, bringing even more pleasure to our lives!
